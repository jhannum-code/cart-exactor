Cart Extract · Bookmarklet

Pull cart artwork in one click.

A one-click bookmarklet that reads the active Shopify cart on ninjatransfers.com and overlays a floating panel with the Upload URL, Width, Height, and Quantity for every line item.

01

Install the bookmarklet

Drag the button below into your bookmarks bar — that's it.
Extract Cart Drag me
Drag the blue button into your browser's bookmarks bar. If the bar is hidden, press ⌘ / Ctrl + Shift + B to show it. Right-click → Bookmark this link also works in most browsers.
Can't drag? Some browsers block javascript: links from being dragged. Create any new bookmark, edit it, and paste the code below into the URL field. Name it Extract Cart. 

        

      

    

  


  
  

    

      
02

      

        
Use it on a cart page

        
Run it from any ninjatransfers.com cart-share URL.

      

    


    

      
    
        
  1. Open the customer's cart-share link (the /a/cart?cart-token=… URL) in your browser. This loads their cart into your session.
    2. 
        
  2. Once the cart page is showing, click the Extract Cart bookmark in your bookmarks bar.
    3. 
        
  3. A floating panel slides in from the right with each line item's upload URL, width, height, and quantity. Click Copy CSV to grab all rows, or copy individual fields.
    4. 
        
  4. Press Esc or click × to close the panel.
    5. 
      

    


    

      What it does under the hood: Fetches /cart.js from the current ninjatransfers.com session (cookies authenticate the request), parses the JSON, walks every line item's properties looking for _Upload (Vector Files Preferred), width, height, and qty (with fuzzy key matching), and renders the results inline. No data leaves the browser.
